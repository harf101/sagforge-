The Maker

Built by hand.
Worn with intent.

SAG FORGE started with a simple frustration: most accessories are made to be sold, not worn. I wanted pieces that could take a beating and still look better for it — chains, rings, and leather goods that age like they were meant to.

Every piece in this collection is designed and tested by hand before it goes into pre-order. Small batches, real materials, no shortcuts. If a piece doesn't earn its place in the collection, it doesn't ship.
